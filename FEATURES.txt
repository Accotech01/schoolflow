================================================================================
 EDUMANAGE NG — SCHOOL MANAGEMENT PLATFORM
 Feature Overview
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EduManage NG is a school management platform built specifically for the
Nigerian curriculum — WAEC grading, the Primary/JSS/SS class structure, and
the academic-session-and-term rhythm schools already run on. It replaces the
spreadsheets, paper attendance registers, printed report cards, and scattered
WhatsApp groups most schools depend on with one system that the school office,
its teachers, its students, and its parents can all log into.

Every feature below is already built and running. Access is role-based: a
School Administrator, a Teacher, a Student, and a Parent each get their own
tailored portal, and each only ever sees the data that belongs to them.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. SCHOOL ADMINISTRATOR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Student & Staff Records
  - Register, edit, and manage full student records: bio-data, guardian
    details, admission numbers, class and subject enrollment
  - Register and manage teacher records and profiles
  - Optionally create a parent portal login at the exact moment a student is
    registered — no separate onboarding step, and siblings automatically
    share one parent account

Academic Structure
  - Create classes (the full Primary 1 through SS 3 structure is built in)
    and subjects, and assign a dedicated class teacher to each class
  - Build the teacher-class-subject assignment matrix for the term
  - Enroll students into classes and subjects per academic session
  - Create an academic session and have all three terms generated
    automatically; advance the active term and set each term's start/end
    dates

End-of-Session Promotions
  - An automatic promotion engine calculates every student's average at
    term/session end and recommends promotion or repetition
  - Review and finalize border-line cases with one click
  - Execute promotions to move every approved student into their new class
    for the new session — no manual re-enrollment

Master Sheet & Class Ranking
  - One view per class: every student's subject-by-subject totals, grand
    total, average, WAEC grade, and class position — sorted best to worst
  - The school's best-performing class for the term is automatically
    identified and highlighted

Attendance Oversight
  - Declare public holidays and midterm breaks once; those dates are
    automatically excluded from every student's attendance record
    school-wide — no student is ever penalized for a day the school was
    closed

Documents, On Demand
  - Generate a formatted, printable admission letter for any student
  - Generate official, print-ready report cards per student per term,
    complete with overall grade and class position

Communication
  - Broadcast announcements to students, teachers, or both — school-wide
    or targeted to a single class
  - A complaints & suggestions inbox collects messages from parents;
    respond directly, and the response is visible back to the parent
  - A live notification inbox surfaces announcements and platform updates

Billing Visibility
  - See exactly how much is currently due and when, calculated
    automatically from the number of actively enrolled students


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. TEACHERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Dashboard
  - Assigned classes and subjects at a glance, plus recent grading activity

Grading
  - Enter Test 1, Test 2, Assignment, Attendance, and Exam scores per
    student, per subject, per term
  - Component weighting is configurable per subject
  - WAEC-style letter grades (A1-F9) are calculated automatically the
    moment a score is saved

Class Teacher Tools (for teachers assigned as a class's homeroom teacher)
  - Mark daily attendance for an entire class in seconds — Present, Late,
    or Absent
  - Attendance can never be marked on a day the school admin has declared
    a holiday or break
  - Convert a term's attendance into a real score, on any scale the
    teacher sets, and post it directly into every student's report card —
    no manual re-entry required

Teaching Materials & Communication
  - Send announcements directly to an entire class
  - Organize lesson notes by week, class, subject, and term
  - Share learning resource links (videos, articles, documents) with
    students
  - Manage a personal profile and receive a live notification inbox


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. STUDENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - A personal dashboard: current class, term average, current grade,
    attendance score, and a clear banner showing which term is active
  - A full results view: per-term, per-subject score breakdown, WAEC
    grade, teacher remarks, and term/session averages
  - Full transparency on standing: every result shows the student's exact
    position within their class
  - One-click PDF download of results — automatically unlocked only once
    every subject has actually been graded, so nothing incomplete ever
    goes out
  - An announcements feed combining school-wide and class-specific updates
  - A resource library of lesson notes and learning links, organized by
    subject
  - A personal profile


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. PARENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - A dedicated login, created by the school the moment their child is
    registered — no separate sign-up process required
  - One account automatically covers every child a parent has at the
    school
  - A dashboard listing every linked child with an at-a-glance summary
  - Full visibility into each child: current class, term-by-term results,
    overall grade, class position, and attendance score
  - A direct line to the school: submit a complaint or suggestion,
    optionally about a specific child, and see the school's response as
    soon as it's addressed


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PLATFORM-WIDE HIGHLIGHTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - True multi-tenancy: every school runs in its own branded space (logo,
    name, unique web address) with fully isolated data
  - Built for the Nigerian curriculum from the ground up: WAEC grading and
    the Primary/JSS/SS class structure are native, not bolted on
  - Role-based access enforced at every layer: four tailored portals, each
    showing only what's relevant to that person
  - Print-ready documents throughout: report cards, admission letters, and
    master sheets are all formatted for clean printing straight from the
    browser
  - A modern, fast experience: pages render with real data immediately —
    no spinners for basic navigation
  - Built-in communication at every level: announcements, direct
    messages, and a complaints channel keep the school office, teachers,
    students, and families connected without a third-party app


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THE PITCH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Today, a typical school's data lives in three places at once: a paper
register in the front office, an Excel sheet a teacher keeps on their own
laptop, and a WhatsApp group parents mute within a week. Report cards are
handwritten or retyped every term. Nobody outside the office knows a
payment is due until it's overdue. Parents find out how their child is
doing only at the next parent-teacher meeting.

EduManage NG puts all of it in one place: one record per student that a
teacher grades, an admin reviews, and a parent can actually see — the
same day it changes. Attendance, results, promotions, communication, and
billing all run through a single system built specifically around how a
Nigerian school already operates, so adopting it doesn't mean changing
how the school works — just how much visibility everyone has into it.
